Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

The Living Word Online ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we handle and how it's used.

1. Information We Store

  • Local Storage on Your Device – We save your reading position, verse highlights, and daily devotion verse directly in your browser so they're available when you return.
  • This information is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers.
  • It will be lost if you clear your browser data, switch devices, or use private browsing mode.
  • Automatic Technical Data – When you visit the site, our hosting provider (Supabase) may log technical details such as your IP address, browser type, and access times. We do not personally link this data to you.

2. How We Use Information

  • To remember your settings, reading state, and highlights.
  • To ensure the site works correctly and fix bugs.

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information like names, email addresses, or payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase – to host and manage scripture topics and related content. When you access this site, Supabase may process technical information (such as your IP address) for basic logging and security purposes. This data is not linked to you personally by us.

5. Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove saved highlights and reading progress.
  • If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the site.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new "Effective Date."
